Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry Ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet Ingredients and stir untill smooth.
  4. pour into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. cool before serving. Frost if desired.
